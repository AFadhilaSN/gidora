GIDORA is a local brand that was formed at the end of 2019. Starting from the anxiety and ambition of 6 young people who had just graduated from vocational high school, to build a business and then the idea to create a brand called GIDORA was sparked. The name GIDORA itself is taken from the word Ghidorah, which is a fictional Japanese monster whose physical characteristics are a dragon monster with many heads. Just like the GIDORA brand, many leaders flap their wings.
